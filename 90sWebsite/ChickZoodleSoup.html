Chicken Zoodle Soup

In this recipe I make some alterations. For the chicken breast, I use 2 cups of shredded rotisserie chicken breast meat (this speeds up the cooking time). Instead of the diced carrots, I use a bag of precut carrot coins, I will use kitchen scissors to cut the coins into halves or quarters depending on their size (also to speed up cooking time). And lastly, I use 2 boxes of spiraled zucchini noodles.
